Philosophy of Love

According to philosophy, there is an infinite number of definitions for love. Some believe that love cant be defined. Some languages dont even have a word for love and disregard as a concept completely. So, to define love would be pointless. Rather than debating what love is, assuming that love is the passionate, intense desire for something (definition of the Greek term Eros), the nature of love and the effects of it on behavior can be analyzed. Acts of love can rage anywhere from a wedding proposal to a crime of passion.
