Definition of Philosophy

The study of the fundamental nature of knowledge, reality, and existence, especially when considered as an academic discipline.


Abstract 1

Philosophy of Love

According to philosophy, there is an infinite number of definitions for love. Some believe that love cant be defined. Some languages dont even have a word for love and disregard as a concept completely. So, to define love would be pointless. Rather than debating what love is, assuming that love is the passionate, intense desire for something (definition of the Greek term Eros), the nature of love and the effects of it on behavior can be analyzed. Acts of love can rage anywhere from a wedding proposal to a crime of passion.

Abstract 3

Philosophy of Epistemology

Epistemology is the study of knowledge. When using this lens its understanding how the knowledge of something, or a lack thereof, can make an impact in a story. Therefore youre able to define what is a belief, the justification of your belief, and what is the truth within a piece of literature. When broken down, a reader can see what knowledge may be a broad agreement, evidence of a belief, or certainty of a belief, helping the reader come to a conclusion on the differences between a belief and the truth.
